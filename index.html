Ümbra's Website

(Because I'm tired of social media)

I'm Ümbra.

(it's pronounced "Ooombra". I replace the Ü with a normal U in most usernames to make it easier to find and tag me.)

I'm an eccentric and eclectic person. I grew up with the internet and spent a lot of time online. I'm well-versed in most online spaces and have a wide variety of interests that influence me and my work. My main mission in life is to try and get as many of the ideas buzzing around my mind out into the world as I can. I have many worlds inside my head - hopefully, one of them appeals to you.

This is a website that acts as a clean repository and portfolio for all of my projects, whether they're visual, musical or written.

What I do

I have a variety of interests in various artforms and produce a lot of different types of creative projects.

My creations can be just about anything, from visual to audio to writing to coding. I'm most dedicated to 'traditional' artforms, as I spend a lot of time using my drawing tablet or producing music in FL studio. I enjoy anything as long as it's a way to express myself or the worlds that I have bottled up.

If you want to learn about the worlds I put together, you can see my worldbuilding plans under the Projects tab (WIP). If you want to use my services for your own projects, you can see my prices under the Commissions tab. You can also Donate to me if you like what you see and simply want to support me.
