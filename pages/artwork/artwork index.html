Artwork

Selections from my beginning to now

2022

Present Year

Much of 2022 was affected by an eating disorder and the subsequent treatment of it (continuing from 2021), so artwork production has been slow - however, I'm proud of what I have drawn in 2022 despite the challenges.

2021

Experimentation with a Drawing Tablet

In 2021 I was extremely fortunate to have a friend who gifted me a drawing tablet. I've used it extensively since - it's the best tool I've used to draw in any medium. However, halfway through 2021 I also started suffering from an eating disorder, which hampered my ability to create./

2020

Subject Changes

You may notice the older years have felt a bit samey in terms of subject matter. I was drawing what I enjoyed, which mostly consisted of bullet hell-shmup characters. In 2020 I found a new community to be a part of, and it opened up my interests. I started drawing a lot of new things that I hadn't before. It was uncomfortable at times, but I think it paid off well.

2019

Some Semblance of Quality

I think 2019 was when my artwork started to pick up. This year was a rocky start, but I grew more confident over the techniques I drew with, and eventually I think I started to apply the things I learned from studying and referencing the subjects I was interested in.

2018

Getting a Grip

Even though my artworks weren't good in the first few years, I had a lot of fun figuring out what worked and what didn't. You might notice some of these artpieces have unusually good anatomy- I was experimenting with using a doll program (DesignDoll) and tracing over poses I made. It didn't make up for my quality, but I did learn a thing or two about anatomy and proportions by using it.

2017

The Beginning

I first started drawing in 2017 after being inspired by the artworks I saw online. At the time the only equipment I had was a mouse, and my skills were naturally subpar, so the early days were tough. However, you can see a lot of development and change in this one year alone.
