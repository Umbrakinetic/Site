Rules and Terms

Please reach me for a commission through one of the contact methods at the footer of the website. A direct message through Discord or Twitter is greatly preferred.

I am a learning artist and while I may take interest in drawing a variety of topics, I may refuse - or refund a commission at any point during the project - for any reason. I am not obligated to undertake a commissioned project nor complete it. In the event of failing to complete a commission, I will refund the client. I may consider a work finished, and while I will make changes for the client's preferences, the client may not be refunded solely due to dissatisfaction with the final result. It is ultimately my decision to refund the client.

I recommend thoroughly investigating my previous artpieces as an example for what one may expect from commissioning me. I am comfortable with drawing several topics, and will learn about new ones should the client want it. However, there are a number of themes and topics I am not comfortable with being commissioned for, for varying reasons:

  • Hate Art
  • Political Art
  • Abuse
  • Complex Character Designs (Your mileage may vary)
  • Heavily Mechanical Themes
  • City/Urban Environments

This is not an exhaustive list and I may refuse a commission based on other themes or topics not listed here.

Commissions which feature more than one character will raise the cost by half of the base price per character - A 2-character line artwork will cost $45 USD, and a 3-character line artwork will cost $60 USD. Furthermore, additional fees may be incurred depending on the nature of the commission - for example, if a character has a complex design, but not complex enough for me to refuse the commission. If you are concerned about additional fees in a commission, please inquire about it.

It is not on the price sheet above, but I can draw some non-abstract backgrounds, usually of natural/fantasy scenery. However, this is a price range on its own (Depends on the commission) and the quality may vary. Please inquire about it if interested.

I also offer commissions with NSFW themes, but details are currently hidden to keep this part of the site accessible. You may use the 18+ portion of the site to learn more (WIP - for the time being, simply inquire by contacting me).

Please note that the above price rates are for an individual basis alone. If you happen to represent an organization in some form (ex. A game studio) Then prices will be raised depending on several factors.
